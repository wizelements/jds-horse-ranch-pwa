## Tips for Success

### Services
- Keep descriptions short and clear (50-200 words)
- Update prices if they change seasonally
- Update age requirements as needed

### Testimonials
- Use real customer names and quotes
- Add 5-10 testimonials for best impact
- Use 5-star ratings for happy customers

### Gallery
- Use high-quality photos (landscape orientation works best)
- Include: horses, riders, scenery, happy customers
- Descriptive titles help customers understand each photo
- 10-15 photos is ideal

### Contact Logs
- Check regularly (at least weekly)
- Export monthly for your records
- Notice patterns (busy days, peak seasons)
